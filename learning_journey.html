üìå Saturday 29 November 2025

Data Analysis Course for beginners: Lecture 03: Excel [Cont.]

  1. COUNTIF: To get how many items achieved the condition
    COUNIF(range, criteria)

    Real life example: how many receipts that a specific sales man done?

  2. COUNTIFS: To get how many items achieved multi conditions at the same time
    COUNIF(range1, criteria1, range2, criteria2, ....)

    Real life example: how many receipts that a specific sales man done? and from a specific product

    3. EXAMPLES
    =COUNTIF(A1:A10, "apple")
    =COUNTIF(B1:B20, ">50")
    =COUNTIF(C1:C100, "<=10")

  3. SUMIF: To get the sum of values related to a criteria

    SUMIF(range, criteria, [sum_range])

  4. SUMIFS: To get sum of values related to multi criteria

    SUMIFS(sum_range, range1, criteria1, range2, criteria2)

  5. FORECAST: To expect the coming value based on the previous values

    FORECAST(x[ex: the month], known_y's [ex: the profits], Known_x's [ex: previous months])

    the equation of FORECAST [Linear Regression]

    FORCAST(x) = bx + a :: b(slope), a(y_intercept)
